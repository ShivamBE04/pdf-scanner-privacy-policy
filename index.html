Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

PDF Scanner ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy.

Information We Collect

The app may access:

  • Camera permission (to scan documents)
  • Storage permission (to save generated PDFs)
  • Advertising ID (to serve relevant ads)

We do not collect personal information, passwords, or payment information.

How Your Data Is Used

All scanned documents and generated PDFs are stored locally on your device.

We do not upload your files to external servers.

Advertising

This app displays ads served by Google Ad Manager. Google may use your device's Advertising ID and other non-personal data to serve relevant advertisements.

The types of ads shown include:

  • Banner ads
  • Interstitial ads
  • Native ads
  • Rewarded ads
  • App open ads

You can opt out of personalized ads by visiting your device's Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

For more information on how Google uses data, visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Analytics & Attribution

We use Apptrove, a mobile measurement platform, to track app installs and in-app events such as document scans, PDF saves, and shares. This helps us understand how users interact with the app and improve our services.

Apptrove may collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID / GAID)
  • App usage events (anonymous)
  • Install source information

For more information, visit: https://apptrove.com/privacy-policy

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Expo
  • Google Play Services
  • Google Ad Manager (advertising)
  • Apptrove (analytics & attribution)

These services may collect anonymous technical data as described in their respective privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal files or scanned documents. Advertising and analytics partners may receive anonymous usage data as described above.

Security

We strive to protect your data and keep all document processing local to your device whenever possible.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed toward children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
